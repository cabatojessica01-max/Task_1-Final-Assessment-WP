Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw

Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw is a traditional Filipino vegetable stew that combines squash and string beans in a rich coconut milk base. It is nutritious and best served with rice.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lbs. squash (kalabasa), cubed
  • 1 bunch string beans (sitaw), cut
  • 3 ounces pork belly
  • 4 cups coconut milk
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • Ginger, sliced
  • Cooking oil
  • Fish sauce and pepper

Method

  1. Heat the cooking oil in a pan. Add the pork belly and cook until brown and slightly crispy.
  2. Add the garlic, onion, and ginger. Sauté until fragrant and the onion softens.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and stir. Cover and bring to a boil.
  4. Lower the heat and let it simmer for about 5 minutes.
  5. Add the squash (kalabasa) and cook for another 5 minutes until slightly tender.
  6. Add the string beans (sitaw) and cook for 5 to 8 minutes until vegetables are cooked.
  7. Season with fish sauce and ground black pepper to taste.
  8. Transfer to a serving plate and serve hot with rice.
